### Ingredients 
- 128g peanut butter
- 8 tablespoons butter (softened)
- 146.5g brown sugar
- 50g granulated sugar
- 6.5g vanilla extract 
<br></br>
- 1 large egg (room temp)
<br></br>
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 152g all purpose flour 

<split>

### Directions 
- Cream together peanut butter, butter, brown sugar, granulated sugar, and vanilla extract
- Beat in 1 large egg
- Mix in flour, baking powder, and salt until combined
- Roll into 2 tbsp balls and place on baking sheet 2 inches apart and bake at **350ºF for 12-13 minutes**

<split>

### Notes
- Usually makes me 13-17 cookies
- Tap the baking sheet onto the edge of the oven a couple times if the cookies look really puffed up, lets them deflate and develop the cracked look?
- Have made multiple times! Soft, chewy, grocery store cookie vibes!
