Oatmeal Cookies

Ingredients

  • 125g all purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (softened)
  • 100g granulated sugar
  • 110g light brown sugar

  • 1 large egg

  • 1tsp vanilla extract
  • 120g oatmeal
  • 180g mix-ins (chocolate chips, nuts, etc)

Directions

  1. Combine flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, and ground cinnamon in a bowl
  2. Cream together butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar in a separte bowl
  3. Beat in 1 large egg and vanilla extract
  4. Mix in flour mixture until just combined
  5. Add oatmeal and mix-ins until just combined, do not overmix
  6. Roll into 2 tbsp balls and place on baking sheet 2 inches apart and bake at 375ºF for 8-10 minutes

Notes

  • Yummy with chocolate chips but also very yummy without
  • Have only ever made once, but tasty! Pretty sweet, would make with less sugar next time
